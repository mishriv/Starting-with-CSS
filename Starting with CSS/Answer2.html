Introduction to Chandrayaan-3

Chandrayaan-3 is the third lunar mission by the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) aimed at exploring the Moon's surface and conducting scientific experiments.

Mission Details

Chandrayaan-3 is equipped with advanced instruments to study the lunar environment, collect data on lunar minerals, and analyze the Moon's geology.

Current Mission Status

As of the latest update, Chandrayaan-3 is on the Moon, and ISRO is actively studying moon south pole surface.
